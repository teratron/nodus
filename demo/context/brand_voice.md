# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Tone

- Friendly and approachable
- Professional but not corporate
- Empathetic and understanding
- Confident without being arrogant

## Language

- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and clear
- Avoid jargon unless speaking to technical audience
- Use "we" to represent the brand, "you" for the customer

## Do

- Acknowledge the customer's perspective
- Offer concrete next steps
- Express genuine interest in helping
- Use the customer's name when available

## Don't

- Use sarcasm or dismissive language
- Make promises we can't keep
- Blame the customer
- Use ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation

## Platform-Specific

- **Twitter**: Concise, casual, emoji OK (sparingly)
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, structured, no emoji
- **Instagram**: Warm, visual language, emoji OK
- **Email**: Formal, thorough, clear CTAs
